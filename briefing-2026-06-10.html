Finally, a correction: the EU AI Act's August 2 high-risk obligations have been deferred to December 2, 2027 under the omnibus political agreement; what binds on August 2, 2026 is the Article 50 transparency set, not the full high-risk regime (Source: Gibson Dunn; Latham & Watkins, 2026).

1. AI Market Trends

The frontier moved, and it moved on Anthropic's side of the board. On June 9, Anthropic announced Claude Fable 5 and Claude Mythos 5, the first models in a Mythos-class tier positioned above the Opus class (Source: Anthropic, June 9, 2026). Fable 5 is the generally available, safety-classified model; Mythos 5 is the same underlying model with some safeguards lifted, released only to a small group of cyberdefenders and infrastructure providers through Project Glasswing (Source: TechCrunch, June 9, 2026; CNBC, June 9, 2026). Day-one availability spanned Anthropic's API, Amazon Bedrock, and GitHub Copilot (Source: AWS News Blog, June 9, 2026; GitHub Changelog, June 9, 2026).

The benchmark claims are strong and self-reported — treat accordingly until independent evaluation lands. Anthropic reports Fable 5 / Mythos 5 at 80.3% on SWE-bench Pro (vs. 58.6% for GPT-5.5), 29.3% on Cognition's FrontierCode Diamond (vs. 13.4% for Opus 4.8 and 5.7% for GPT-5.5), and 1932 on GDPval-AA (vs. 1890 for Opus 4.8, 1769 for GPT-5.5) (Source: Anthropic, June 9, 2026, as reported by VentureBeat and news.bitcoin.com). These are self-reported with no independent verification available yet, and should not be presented as settled. The genuinely notable number for a practitioner is price: the model launched at less than half the Mythos Preview rate (Source: Anthropic; news.bitcoin.com, June 9, 2026).

On billing: the June 15 change moves Claude Agent SDK, claude -p, and Claude Code GitHub Actions onto a separate metered monthly credit billed at full API rates, while Claude.ai chat, interactive terminal Claude Code, and Claude Cowork keep drawing from normal subscription limits, unchanged (Source: TechTimes, June 2, 2026; DigitalApplied, 2026). Confirm against Anthropic's primary billing documentation before quoting client costs.

On regulation: under the AI omnibus (political agreement May 7, 2026), high-risk obligations for stand-alone Annex III systems are deferred to December 2, 2027, and Annex I embedded systems to August 2, 2028 (Source: Gibson Dunn, 2026; Latham & Watkins, 2026). What still takes effect August 2, 2026 is the Article 50 transparency regime and the Act's general applicability (Source: European Commission, accessed June 10, 2026). The omnibus takes legal effect only on formal adoption and publication; plan for the original deadline as a contingency.

What this means for practitioners. Fable 5's built-in fallback-to-Opus-4.8 safeguard is a concrete artifact of governed model selection: a good implementation policy should document which model answers which request, and what happens on a flagged query. The EU correction is its own lesson — a date everyone circled moved, which is exactly why a posture assessment confirms classification and current effective dates rather than relying on last quarter's calendar.
