1. AI Market Trends

Claude Opus 4.7 released — April 16, 2026. Anthropic shipped Opus 4.7 across all surfaces: Claude.ai, the API, Amazon Bedrock, Google Cloud Vertex AI, and Microsoft Foundry. The headline numbers: SWE-bench Verified at 87.6% (up from 80.8%), SWE-bench Pro at 64.3% (up from 53.4%, leapfrogging GPT-5.4 at 57.7% and Gemini 3.1 Pro at 54.2%), GPQA Diamond at 94.2%, and CursorBench at 70% (up from 58%). Vision capacity tripled — images now accepted up to 2,576 pixels on the long edge (~3.75 megapixels), enabling small-text reading in screenshots, dense UI mockup analysis, and high-resolution document parsing. New features include: xhigh effort level (between high and max), /ultrareview command in Claude Code, auto mode for Max users, and task budgets in public beta. Opus 4.7 is also the first Claude model shipping with automated detection and blocking for prohibited cybersecurity uses — a direct downstream application of Mythos Preview's vulnerability-discovery capabilities. Pricing unchanged: $5/$25 per million tokens. (Source: Anthropic, "Introducing Claude Opus 4.7," April 16, 2026; CNBC, April 16, 2026; The Next Web, April 16, 2026)
