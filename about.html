About Malaniru

A boutique, fully remote AI implementation agency that builds and maintains Claude Cowork environments for professionals and small organizations nationwide — with honest guidance on what agentic AI can and can’t do for the work in front of you.

Who we are

Malaniru guides professionals from AI curiosity to AI capability with a curated, white-glove Claude Cowork implementation tailored to their actual workflow. The work is hands-on: assess where an organization stands, put governance in place, build the team’s real skill with the tools, and leave behind a working environment plus the documented practices to run it.

Alongside client delivery, Malaniru curates and publishes practitioner-level content on agentic AI — what holds up under real use, and what doesn’t. The goal is straightforward: help people make informed decisions about AI adoption without the hype, including the decision not to adopt something.

Who runs it

Malaniru is led by Mark Andrew Harris, Principal. Mark brings 26 years in data analysis and software development — application and mobile development, data analysis, SQL, and Excel across the full stack, and a long habit of translating between the technical and the practical for people who don’t live in code.

On the leadership side, he has run engineering teams at the team-lead, manager, and director levels — managing 15+ developers across concurrent projects, scaling teams from a handful to twenty-plus, and serving as Product Owner and Solution Architect. That background shapes how Malaniru is built: small, deliberate, and accountable for what it ships.

The path here wasn’t a straight line. A US Coast Guard veteran with hands-on operational experience before a long career in software, Mark brings an operational discipline and a tolerance for real-world constraints that show up in how engagements are scoped and run — practical over theoretical, every time.

Where we focus

Malaniru’s primary vertical is the philanthropic sector — grantmaking foundations and the organizations that serve them. The compliance, data migration, and workflow challenges in that space are a strong fit for what agentic AI actually does well, and for the careful, human-in-the-loop approach Malaniru takes to deploying it. The firm also draws on prior experience across enterprise software, real estate and mortgage technology, and agency web development.

  • Boutique by design

    Selective clientele, high standards, and engagements sized to the organization — not enterprise over-engineering.

  • Accuracy first

    A wrong answer is worse than no answer. Every deliverable is built to flag uncertainty rather than paper over it.

  • Human in the loop

    AI handles the routine; people keep control of anything that matters. Irreversible actions always get human review.

  • You keep the capability

    Every engagement leaves behind a documented environment and the practices to run it — not a dependency on us.
