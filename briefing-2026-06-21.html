Third, the backdrop. A Reuters Institute figure cited this weekend puts Claude at roughly 8.2% of global chatbot web visits and 12.5% in the US. Anthropic's confidential IPO filing from June 1 remains the financial context, with reported valuation and revenue figures sourced from trade press rather than audited disclosures.

What This Means for You. The partner-network race is the most important item this week for anyone choosing or being an implementation partner. And the Korea wave is a useful proof point that serious enterprises are deploying Claude Cowork and Code on the available, stable tiers — the conservative posture worth defaulting to.
